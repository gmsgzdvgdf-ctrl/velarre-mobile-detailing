Why Velarre

Precision, Discretion, and a Genuine Love of Fine Automobiles

Velarre Mobile Detailing was founded to bring a level of care usually reserved for concours-level shops directly to Highlands County drivers — at home, at work, or trackside.

Based minutes from Sebring International Raceway, we understand exotic and performance vehicles the way owners do: as machines worth protecting, not just cleaning.

Every appointment is treated with the same discretion and precision, whether it's a daily driver or a track-ready exotic.
